About Krishikaam

We are dedicated to bringing the power of modern technology to every small farmer in India, ensuring a future that is profitable, sustainable, and secure.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower the 100 million smallholder farmers of India. We bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology by providing easy-to-use tools that increase efficiency, boost profits, and promote safe agricultural practices. From drone spraying to direct market access, we've built a single platform that addresses the core challenges farmers face every day.

Krishikaam was born from a desire to make a real difference. After seeing firsthand the struggles of rural farming communities, we knew that technology, if made accessible, could be a powerful force for change. We started with a single drone in one village and have grown into a nationwide network of farmers, operators, and local agents, all working together to cultivate a better future.

Our Core Values

Farmer First

Every tool we build and every service we offer is designed to create tangible value for the farmer.

Accessible Innovation

We make complex technology simple and affordable, ensuring it can be used by anyone, anywhere.

Building Trust

We operate with transparency and integrity, building lasting relationships with our community.
