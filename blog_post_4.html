Krishi Jal: On-Demand Water Access for Every Farmer

By Krishikaam Team | June 29, 2025

For a farmer, water is life. Access to reliable irrigation can be the difference between a bountiful harvest and a devastating loss. However, for millions of small farmers in India, consistent water access is a major challenge. The high cost of setting up and maintaining a borewell is often prohibitive, leading to dependency on unpredictable monsoons or expensive water tankers.

Krishikaam introduces Krishi Jal, an innovative solution designed to democratize water access and empower every farmer with the water they need, exactly when they need it.

The Problem with Traditional Water Access

In many villages, a few farmers who can afford borewells often control the local water supply. This can lead to high prices, unfair distribution, and sometimes, community disputes. Farmers without their own water source are left vulnerable, unable to irrigate their crops at critical stages of growth.

How Krishi Jal Works: Simple, Fair, and Transparent

Our Krishi Jal service transforms existing borewells into community assets. We partner with borewell owners and install a smart, QR-code-based controller on their pump.

  • Simple QR Scan: Any farmer needing water can simply scan the QR code at the borewell using the Krishikaam app or with the help of a Krishi Mitra agent.
  • Pay-Per-Use Model: Instead of paying a high, fixed rate, farmers pay only for the amount of time they use the pump. The transaction is handled digitally via UPI, ensuring complete transparency.
  • Automated Operation: Once the payment is confirmed, the pump automatically turns on for the selected duration. There's no need for manual intervention or negotiation.
  • Benefits for All: The farmer gets water at an affordable, predictable rate. The borewell owner earns a steady income from their asset without any hassle. This creates a win-win situation that strengthens the entire community.

The Impact of Reliable Water

"Last year, my crops suffered because I couldn't get water at the right time. This year, with Krishi Jal, I scanned the code on my neighbor's borewell whenever I needed it. My yield has doubled, and there are no more arguments about water in the village." - A Farmer from Bihar.

Krishi Jal is more than just a service; it's a step towards water security and community harmony. By leveraging simple technology, we are breaking down barriers and ensuring that this vital resource is available to all. It's another way Krishikaam is using innovation to build a more resilient and prosperous future for Indian agriculture.
