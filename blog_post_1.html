The Drone Revolution: How Technology is Making Farming Safer and More Profitable

By Krishikaam Team | June 25, 2025

For generations, farming in India has been defined by hard manual labor. One of the most challenging and dangerous tasks is the spraying of pesticides and fertilizers. Farmers often carry heavy tanks on their backs, walking for hours under the sun, and exposing themselves directly to harmful chemicals. This traditional method is not only a risk to their health but is also inefficient, often leading to uneven application and wastage of expensive inputs.

But a revolution is taking flight, and it's powered by drones.

The Krishikaam Advantage: Speed, Precision, and Safety

At Krishikaam, we are harnessing the power of drone technology to tackle these age-old problems. Our drone spraying service is designed to be a game-changer for the small farmers of India. Here’s how:

  • Unmatched Speed: A task that takes hours of manual labor can be completed in minutes. Our drones can cover an entire acre of land in just 7 minutes. This frees up the farmer's valuable time for other important activities.
  • Superior Coverage: Drones provide a uniform and consistent spray across the entire field, reaching every part of the plant. This ensures that crops get the protection they need, leading to healthier plants and higher yields.
  • Reduced Wastage: With precision application, our drones use up to 40% less water and 20-30% fewer chemicals compared to manual methods. This not only saves farmers money but is also better for the environment.
  • Safety First: This is the most crucial benefit. By using drones, farmers are no longer in direct contact with potentially hazardous chemicals. They can operate the service from a safe distance, protecting their long-term health.

More Than Just a Service

We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've made our drone service incredibly affordable, starting at just ₹59 per kathha. Our network of trained, local Krishi Mitra agents ensures that booking a service is as simple as making a phone call, even without a smartphone.

The drone revolution is not just about flying machines; it's about empowering farmers with tools that make their work more profitable, their lives easier, and their health a priority. Join us in embracing this new era of smart, safe, and sustainable agriculture.
